Get started with Dontprint

You're almost ready to use Dontprint. Follow these three simple steps to get started.

Step 1: Download k2pdfopt

Dontprint automatically optimizes the layout of articles so that you can read them comfortably on your e-reader. This is done with a program called k2pdfopt developed by William Menninger.

Step 3: Set up document transfer

Choose how Dontprint should transfer documents to your e-reader.
Dontprint can send articles by e-mail directly to your e-reader. To use this feature, you need an e-mail address that ends in "@gmail.com" (why?). Dontprint takes your privacy serious and gains only very limited access to your Gmail account (more information on privacy).
  1. If you don't already have a Gmail account, create one for free.
  2. If your e-reader is an Amazon Kindle you need to allow Dontprint to send documents to it.
    Go to "Manage Your Kindle" on amazon.com and add your "...@gmail.com" address to the "Approved Personal Document E-mail List" (see image below; help).
  3. Tell Dontprint your e-reader's e-mail address (what's that?):
    Dontprint transfers documents wirelessly to your e-reader by e-mail. If your e-reader is an Amazon Kindle you find the e-mail address that is tied to your Kindle in the section "Send-to-Kindle E-Mail Settings" on the "Manage Your Kindle" page (see image below; further help). If your e-reader is not from Amazon then please consult the manual to find out if it can receive documents by e-mail.

Congratulations

You are now ready to use Dontprint. Go ahead and try it out:

  1. Go to the website of a publisher.
  2. Log in or use your institution's proxy.
  3. Navigate to an article. A Dontprint icon should appear (see screenshot). Click it!

Dontprint will download the PDF file of the article, optimize the layout for a small screen, and send it to your e-reader.
